About the Project

Women's safety requires proactive, data-driven solutions rather than reactive measures. This system analyses crime data, location infrastructure, and time-based patterns to identify unsafe areas in Thrissur. It assigns ML-predicted safety scores and visualises risk zones on interactive heatmaps — helping users make informed travel decisions before they step out.
